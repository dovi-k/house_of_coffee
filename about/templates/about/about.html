About Us

Little bit about us. We've opened our door in 2019 just before the pandemic hit and with everyones lifestyles drastically changing, we've realised how important it is to have something familiar and accesible all the time so that's how our little coffee shop slowly started transitioning into the virtual space.

We know very well how important it is a good cup of coffee in the morning and we make it our mission to serve excellence with every single cup. Our new delivery services allows you to enjoy high quality cup of coffee wherever you'd be.

Don't forget to explore our Shop area, where you can find gurment coffee beans, hot chocolates or speciality teas.
